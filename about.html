About Us

Learn about our story, our mission, and the team behind Serenity Care Home

Our Story

Established in 1995, Serenity Care Home was founded by Dr. Elizabeth Morgan, a geriatric specialist with a vision to create a care facility that truly felt like home. After witnessing the challenges faced by elderly patients in traditional care settings, Dr. Morgan set out to build a place where dignity, compassion, and personalized care were at the forefront.

Starting with just 15 residents, our care home quickly gained a reputation for exceptional care and a warm, family-like atmosphere. Over the years, we've grown to accommodate more residents while maintaining our commitment to personalized care.

Today, Serenity Care Home stands as a testament to Dr. Morgan's vision, providing a nurturing environment where residents can thrive, maintain their independence, and enjoy their golden years with dignity and joy.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, person-centered care that enhances the quality of life for our residents, respects their dignity and independence, and brings peace of mind to their families.

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy, kindness, and genuine care for our residents' wellbeing.

Dignity

We respect each resident's individuality, privacy, and right to make choices about their care.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in care, continuously improving our services and facilities.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and connection among residents, staff, families, and the wider community.
