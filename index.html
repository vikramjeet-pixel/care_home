About Serenity Care Home

Established in 1995, Serenity Care Home has been providing exceptional care for over 25 years. Our purpose-built facility offers a perfect blend of professional care and homely comfort.

We understand that choosing a care home is a significant decision, which is why we focus on creating a nurturing environment where residents can thrive.
