Our Services

Comprehensive care services tailored to meet individual needs

Personalized Care Approach

At Serenity Care Home, we understand that each resident has unique needs and preferences. Our approach to care is centered around creating personalized care plans that respect individual choices while ensuring the highest standards of health and wellbeing.

Our qualified staff work closely with residents and their families to develop and continuously review these care plans, adapting them as needs change over time.

Our Care Services

Residential Care

Our core service provides comfortable accommodation, nutritious meals, and 24-hour support with personal care needs, all within a warm and homely environment.

  • 24-hour support and supervision
  • Assistance with daily activities
  • Medication management
  • Regular health monitoring

Dementia Care

Specialized care for residents living with dementia, delivered by staff trained in person-centered dementia care approaches.

  • Dementia-friendly environment
  • Specialized activities and therapies
  • Staff trained in dementia care
  • Support for families

Respite Care

Short-term care options for those recovering from illness or injury, or to provide a break for regular caregivers.

  • Flexible duration stays
  • Full access to all facilities
  • Personalized care plans
  • Smooth transition support

Palliative Care

Compassionate end-of-life care focused on comfort, dignity, and quality of life, with emotional support for both residents and their families.

  • Pain and symptom management
  • Emotional and spiritual support
  • Family involvement
  • Dignified and peaceful environment

Nutritional Support

Delicious, balanced meals prepared by our in-house chefs, with special diets and nutritional needs accommodated.

  • Personalized meal plans
  • Special dietary requirements
  • Nutritional assessment
  • Hydration monitoring

Wellness Programs

Regular activities and therapies designed to promote physical health, mental wellbeing, and social engagement.

  • Gentle exercise classes
  • Cognitive stimulation
  • Social activities
  • Therapeutic recreation

Dental Services

Optometry

Medication Management

Fee Structure

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Our fees are structured to provide excellent value for the comprehensive care we offer.

Our fees vary depending on the level of care required and the type of accommodation chosen. All fees include:

  • Fully furnished private room
  • All meals and snacks
  • 24-hour care and support
  • Laundry and housekeeping
  • Activities and entertainment
  • Utilities and maintenance

We accept both private funding and local authority funding (subject to assessment). We're also happy to provide guidance on funding options and benefits that may be available to you.

For a personalized quote and to discuss your specific needs, please contact our admissions team.
